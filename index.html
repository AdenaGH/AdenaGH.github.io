Ok...What Now?

Think about everything you just learned next time you go to the grocery store. Instead of just grabbing the first bag of apples you see, ask yourself if you are getting the best value based on the information above. Will the recipe you are making taste the same if you buy canned apples instead of fresh? Could you switch it for pears since you know they have a higher yield? How many cups of sliced apples do you need for that pie, and can you acheive that amount with less apples than what is in the pre-packaged bag? These are just examples, but we implore you to take your newfound knowledge and apply it to your purchases. Learning about how your produce is priced can make you a more proactive consumer and can help you ensure you are getting the most bang for your buck at the grocery store.
